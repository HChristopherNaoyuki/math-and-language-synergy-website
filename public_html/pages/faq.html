Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs and services

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in our courses through our online enrollment form on the Enrollment page. Simply select your desired course, fill out the required information, and submit the form. Our team will contact you within 24-48 hours to confirm your enrollment and provide next steps.

Alternatively, you can visit our campus during business hours to enroll in person.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including:

  • Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Bank transfers
  • Payment plans (for semester-long courses)
  • Cash payments (in-person only)

Payment plans can be arranged on a case-by-case basis. Please contact our administration office for more information.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online classes for most of our programs. Our online classes are conducted through a secure learning management system that includes:

  • Live video sessions with instructors
  • Interactive whiteboards and collaboration tools
  • Recorded sessions for later review
  • Digital resources and materials
  • Online assessments and feedback

You can choose your preferred format during enrollment.

What is the typical class size?

We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention and effective learning:

  • Group classes: 8-12 students maximum
  • Semi-private sessions: 3-5 students
  • Private tutoring: One-on-one instruction

Our small class sizes allow instructors to provide individualized feedback and support to each student.

Can I switch courses after enrolling?

Yes, you can switch courses within the first two weeks of the semester, subject to availability. Please contact our administration office to discuss your options. There may be a course difference fee if the new course has a different tuition rate.

After the first two weeks, course changes may still be possible but are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

What technical requirements are needed for online classes?

For optimal experience in our online classes, we recommend:

  • A computer with a reliable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps download speed)
  • Webcam and microphone for participation
  • Updated web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
  • Our learning platform works on most devices, but a computer or tablet is recommended for the best experience

Detailed technical requirements will be provided upon enrollment.

Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?

Yes, we offer a limited number of scholarships and financial aid options for qualified students. These include:

  • Academic merit scholarships
  • Need-based financial aid
  • Payment plans
  • Early enrollment discounts

Please contact our administration office for more information about eligibility and application procedures.

What is the duration of your courses?

Course durations vary depending on the program:

  • Standard language courses: 12-16 weeks per semester
  • Intensive language programs: 4-8 weeks
  • Mathematics courses: 10-14 weeks per semester
  • Workshops and special programs: 1-2 days to 4 weeks

Specific course durations are listed in the course descriptions on our Services page.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help you with any additional questions you may have about our programs and services.
