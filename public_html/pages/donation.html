Frequently Asked Questions

Why Bitcoin only?

We accept Bitcoin donations to minimize transaction fees and provide a borderless way for our global community to support our mission.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, we are a registered educational institution and donations may be tax-deductible in your jurisdiction. We provide donation receipts for all contributions.

How will my donation be used?

100% of donations go directly to supporting our educational programs, student scholarships, and learning resource development.
